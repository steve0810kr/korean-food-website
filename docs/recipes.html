Steps

  7. Mix everything well before eating.

Tteokbokki is a famous Korean street food: chewy rice cakes in a sweet and spicy red sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200 g tteok (Korean rice cakes)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1‚Äì2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp sugar

  2. Add rice cakes and cook until they become soft and the sauce starts to thicken.

  4. Serve hot, with a boiled egg on top if you like.
